Lego Sentry Powered by a Raspberry Pi

Ever since I was a kid, I was fascinated by mechanical sentries. Machines that would look at you, following your every move.

Using LEGO as the building blocks for this project, I created a chassis that can handle the weight of the two motors move given a limited range of 180 degrees.

I created kickstand for the Pi by balancing it on two beams that pressed into the Pi and held it in place.

Equipment

  • Raspberry Pi 4 Model B
  • LEGO build HAT
  • LEGO Medium Linear Motor
