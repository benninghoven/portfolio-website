2019 Dell Optiplex 3070

⚡️Average Power Consumption: Unknown

I got this machine from my internship on the summer of '22. It was a workstation that was destined for the recycling bin. I installed a NIC Intel PRO/1000 PT Dual Port Server Adapter in it and turned it into a powerful PFSense router.

HP 2910al-48G-PoE Switch (J9148A)

  • ⚡️Average Power Consumption: 90W
  • 48 Gigabit Ports
  • PoE Capable
  • Age: Unknown

I got this switch from my internship on the summer of '22.

In it's previous life, it served as the main switch for a dentist's office.

It was getting replaced with a newer model, so I got to take it home.

As soon as it booted up, a loud fan noise filled the room. I quickly opened it up and replaced the fans with some Noctua NF-A4x20 fans. The decibal level went from 58dB to under 32dB.

Raspberry Pi CM4

  • ⚡️Average Power Consumption: ??
  • Age: 2 years

I followed this guide to install the UniFi Controller on my Raspberry Pi CM4.

Why did I not just buy a Cloud Key? Because MONEY. A cloud key is very expensive for what it is, and a simple RPI can do the job perfectly. Why a CM4? Because this was during the great Pi shortage of 2022, and I could not find a regular Pi 4. The CM4 is cooled with this case from amazon. This could just as well be inside of a virtual machine, but this is vital for the network to function, so I wanted it to be on a dedicated machine.
